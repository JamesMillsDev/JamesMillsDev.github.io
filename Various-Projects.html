Projects

Bunbury Forum Christmas VR: 
This was a Christmas VR experience for a shopping center in Western Australia, where people could experience flying over the town of Bunbury, off to and through the village of the North Pole, before returning home.

Working as the lead programmer with Tunacorn Studios, I was responsible for the majority of the development process. I built the level transitioning, networking and event timing systems. I also integrated assets such as Curvy Splines and FinalIK.

Red Cartel:I worked on various client projects for companies such as the QATAR world cup, Optus and Shell. I primarily worked as a VR and mobile programmer, with hardware such as HTC Vive, HTC Focus 3 and Oculus Quest. I worked with both the Unity Networking (UNET) API and Mirror to implement networking into multiple projects. I built systems that read from remote live updating data and worked with both Perforce and Git version control systems.
