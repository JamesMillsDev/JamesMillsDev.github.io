Hey, I’m James – a game developer with 6+ years of experience working in Unity (C#) and Unreal Engine (C++). Experienced with VR. Passionate about doing frameworks, AI, networking. Enjoy dabbling in every part of the game development pipeline, from modelling to shaders to physics.

I’ve always been obsessed with the magic behind games – especially procedural generation, which constantly pulls me down rabbit holes of systems designing. I grew up on PS2 platformers and story-driven games, like Jak and Daxter, Ratchet and Clank, and Kingdom Hearts, and that love still fuels the kind of experiences I aim to create: ones that are fun, immersive, and leave a lasting impact.

I’m always up for collaborating on something creative, building tools that make life easier for developers, or chatting about game dev over coffee (or cat pics).
