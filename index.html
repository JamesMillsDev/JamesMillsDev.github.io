< /About Me >

Hi, I’m James — a game developer with over six years of experience working in Unity (C#) and Unreal Engine (C++). I have a strong background in VR development and a deep interest in building frameworks, AI systems, and networking solutions. I enjoy exploring every part of the game development pipeline, from modeling and shaders to physics and gameplay systems.
 
I’ve always been obsessed with the magic behind games – especially procedural generation, which constantly pulls me down rabbit holes of systems designing. I grew up on PS2 platformers and story-driven games, and that love still fuels the kind of experiences I aim to create: ones that are fun, immersive, and memorable.

I’m always up for collaborating on something creative, building tools that make life easier for developers, or chatting about game dev over tea (or cat pics).
